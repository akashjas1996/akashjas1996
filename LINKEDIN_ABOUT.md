I founded Wooble because India's hiring system still rewards college brand and credentials — not what people can actually do.

Wooble is a proof-of-work hiring marketplace. Early-career builders — developers, designers, data analysts, product managers, and MBA graduates — earn credibility by completing real company briefs and hackathons, getting scored by domain reviewers, and publishing verified portfolios. Companies hire on demonstrated work, not paper résumés.

What we've built:
→ 2.24L+ registered candidates across India
→ 200+ company partners, from startups to enterprises like Tata Steel
→ 5,877+ placements facilitated
→ MoU with BPUT covering 150+ affiliated colleges
→ Rs. 7 Cr+ ARR run rate
